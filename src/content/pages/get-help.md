Habitat for Humanity of Central Lane helps neighbors build and preserve affordable housing through homeownership and minor exterior home repair programs.

## Homeownership

**Please note that this program is not accepting new applications.**

Habitat for Humanity of Central Lane helps low-to-moderate-income families achieve affordable homeownership by building homes, which are then sold with affordable mortgages. Applicants must demonstrate a need for housing, willingness to partner, including 200-250 hours of participation, and ability to repay the mortgage.

[Learn about homeownership](homeownership/)

## Minor Home Repair

**NOTE: The Minor Home Repair Program is not accepting applications at this time.**

HFHCL works to assist low-income homeowners, including veterans, seniors, and disabled with minor exterior home repairs with a primary focus on accessibility.

[Learn about minor home repair](minor-home-repair/)
