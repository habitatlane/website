## Cars for Homes

Habitat for Humanity of Central Lane's Cars for Homes vehicle donation program accepts cars, trucks, RVs, boats, and motorcycles, running or not. Towing is free.

[Learn about Cars for Homes](cars-for-homes/)

## In-Kind Donations

In-kind donations help Habitat for Humanity of Central Lane build homes and make repairs by providing essential materials and services that reduce overall project costs.

[Learn about in-kind donations](in-kind/)
