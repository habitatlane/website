Volunteering is a cornerstone to our organization. There are many ways to lend a hand, and no experience is necessary.

## Volunteer with Habitat Central Lane

The first place to start is the volunteer page, which links to Habitat Central Lane's volunteer portal and explains the ReStore volunteer process.

[Learn about volunteering](volunteer/)

## Volunteer at the ReStore

Because the ReStore is a public facing retail environment, prospective volunteers must meet with the ReStore Director and Volunteer Coordinator first. Please call to set up an appointment: [541-741-1701](tel:5417411701).

[Please go to the ReStore website to learn more.](https://habitatlanerestore.org/volunteer/)
