## Monthly giving

Our monthly giving community makes a lasting impact all year long. Becoming a recurring donor provides reliable, sustained support that allows Habitat Central Lane to plan ahead, respond quickly to emerging needs, and grow programs with confidence.

How to donate monthly:

- Send a check
- Call Habitat Central Lane to set up an ACH payment
- [Go online and use your debit or credit card](https://wl.donorperfect.net/weblink/WebLink.aspx?name=E169686&id=1)
