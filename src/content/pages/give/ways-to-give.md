## Planned Giving

Planned giving is a way to both preserve your family's financial future and leave a charitable legacy for years to come. There are a variety of planned giving options available, and the type of planned gift you choose depends on your current and future financial needs as well as the assets you would like to donate. A financial planner can help you develop a giving plan designed to benefit the people and organizations that matter most to you. This kind of generosity can be implemented while alive or after your death.

Planned giving can include gifts of:

- Real Estate
- Property
- Cash
- Stocks
- Investments
- Mutual Funds
- Life Insurance
- Beneficiary Proceeds
- Annuity Proceeds

Benefits of planned giving include giving more than your current income allows, giving an asset other than cash, receiving tax benefits, and receiving income for a set number of years or for life. Please contact Annie Leadingham at [541-741-1707 ext. 1103](tel:5417411707) to discuss.

## Monthly Giving

Our monthly giving community makes a lasting impact all year long. Becoming a recurring donor provides reliable, sustained support that allows us to plan ahead, respond quickly to emerging needs, and grow our programs with confidence. Even a small monthly gift adds up to meaningful change over time, helping us reach more people and create deeper impact in the communities we serve. It's simple, flexible, and one of the most effective ways to stand with Habitat Central Lane making a difference every single month.

How to donate monthly:

- Send a check
- Call us to set up an ACH payment
- [Go online and use your debit or credit card](https://wl.donorperfect.net/weblink/WebLink.aspx?name=E169686&id=1)

You can also support Habitat Central Lane through [Cars for Homes](../cars-for-homes/) and [In-Kind Donations](../in-kind/).
