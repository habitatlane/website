## Donate a Car in the Habitat Central Lane Service Area

**Our service area goes from Coburg to Cottage Grove and from Blue River to Noti, including large swaths of rural Lane County as well as the Eugene/Springfield metro area.**

Donating a car in our service area is one of the most impactful, and easiest ways to support affordable homeownership in our shared community. Habitat for Humanity of Central Lane's Cars for Homes vehicle donation program accepts cars, trucks, RVs, boats, and motorcycles, running or not. Towing is free. Your donation may also qualify for a tax donation.

Every vehicle donated to Cars for Homes in our area generates funding that goes directly toward building and repairing homes for local community members.

Vehicle donations may be made online through the [donation wizard](https://www.cardonationwizard.com/habitat-for-humanity/donate/donate-a-car-build-a-home-in-your-local-community.html?__cr_user=50724acf12d8c1ea6c5b043df2fe5106&__cr_session=302d5809496678290f4800b21c5931fc) or by calling [1-877-277-4344](tel:18772774344).
