Volunteering is a cornerstone to our organization. There are many ways to lend a hand, and no experience is necessary.

The first place to start is to register on our volunteer portal, with exception to the ReStore: [https://habitatlane.galaxydigital.com](https://habitatlane.galaxydigital.com)

## Volunteering at the ReStore

Because the ReStore is a public facing retail environment, prospective volunteers must meet with the ReStore Director and Volunteer Coordinator first prior to setting up an account on our volunteer portal. Please call to set up an appointment: [541-741-1701](tel:5417411701).

[Please go to the ReStore website to learn more.](https://habitatlanerestore.org/volunteer/)
