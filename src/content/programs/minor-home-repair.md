Minor Home Repair supports homeowners with practical repairs that improve
safety, accessibility, and livability.

Projects may include critical fixes, exterior repairs, or modifications that
help residents remain in their homes and communities. Habitat staff review
requests, available funding, and project fit before work begins.

Staff will update this page as program availability, eligibility, and intake
details change.
