Habitat homeownership is a partnership between future homeowners, volunteers,
donors, and Habitat for Humanity of Central Lane.

Partner families help build their homes alongside volunteers, complete
homebuyer education, and purchase their homes with an affordable mortgage.
The program is designed for households who need safe, stable housing and are
ready to take the next step toward owning a home.

Program details, application windows, and eligibility information will be kept
current here by Habitat staff.
